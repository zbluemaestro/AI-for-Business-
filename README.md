### Phase 2 — AI Marketing (Weeks 4–5)
- **Week 4** — AI marketing courses (Alison, Salesforce, Great Learning) + content calendar lab ⚡

### Phase 3 — AI Agents & Automation (Weeks 6–9)
- **Week 6–7** — Build AI agents with n8n, no code + live automation lab ⚡
- **Week 8–9** — Microsoft's GenAI curriculum + Andrew Ng's Generative AI for Everyone

### Phase 4 — Freelancing & Online Income (Weeks 10–11)
- **Week 10** — Fiverr & Upwork setup + publish 3 live gigs ⚡

- **Week 12–13** — Kelley Business School GenAI badge + Google AI Essentials
- **Week 14** — Final lab: land your first real client ⚡

## ⚡ Practical Labs

7 real hands-on projects embedded throughout the roadmap — not exercises, actual deliverables:

1. **First AI prompt session** — calibrate your trust in AI tools
2. **Personal landing page** — built with bolt.new, zero code
3. **30-day content calendar** — ready to sell on Fiverr day one
4. **Live n8n automation** — connect your Gmail, Notion, or forms
5. **3 Fiverr gigs published** — with Claude-written descriptions
6. **Digital product on Gumroad** — your first passive income asset
7. **First real client** — free automation for a local business → paid second one
